#### 1. 定义
&emsp;名词是用来命名人、事物、地点和抽象事物（A noun is the name of a person or thing）.
> * 人称：john,sister,father
> * 事物：water,air,computer
> * 地点：London,theater
> * 抽象事物：love,hapiness,imagination,hope
